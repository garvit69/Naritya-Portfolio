Hey there.

As an innovative architectural designer, I bring a fresh perspective to my work. With extensive experience in BIM 360 and computational design, I approach each project with a data-driven mindset, leveraging cutting-edge technology to optimize design solutions with an iterative approach.

My final thesis project, "Polymorphous Transit Matrix," and published research paper, "Geometry Augmentation for Spatial Development to Optimize Pedestrian Flow and Circulation," showcase my expertise in creating spatial development frameworks that prioritize functionality and efficiency while also promoting aesthetic excellence. I am proud to have contributed to the discourse on sustainable urban design, transportation, and infrastructure planning, and I am constantly pushing the boundaries of what is possible in this field.

My success in collaborating with teams to ensure successful project delivery is a testament to my skills in project management, BIM modeling, coordination, and client communication. I have a proven track record of delivering high-quality projects on time and within budget, all while keeping a keen eye on detail and design excellence.

In addition to my work on traditional architectural projects, I have experience in design competitions, mentoring, and design consulting. I am passionate about sharing my knowledge and expertise with others, and I am always eager to collaborate with like-minded individuals and organizations.

If you're searching for an architectural designer with a unique blend of technical expertise, creative vision, and a collaborative spirit, then look no further. I am excited to explore new ideas and technologies in the field of architecture and contribute to the creation of sustainable, functional, and aesthetically pleasing data-driven solutions.
